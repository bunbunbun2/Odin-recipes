Protein Oat Cakes Recipe



Description

For when me go gym. Me no want to make bfast in morning, me want quick sweet snack. But cake bad so me make protein cake.

Ingredients
  • 2 cups oats
  • 2 cups of milk
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 scoops salted caramel baking posder
  • 1 cup of blueberries (frozen or non)
recipe
  1. Grab a bowl
  2. Put 2 cups of oats in a bowl and mix with 1.5 cups of milk
  3. Add baking powder, protein powder and blueberries
  4. Put in baking tray and bake in oven that's been preheated to 180c for 40min
